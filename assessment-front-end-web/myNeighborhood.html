My Neighborhood

My neighborhood is very beautiful if I do say so myself, not just because I live in it! Homes here have a modern look and what I like most about my peaceful neighborhood is, people here care about their community and are interested in keeping it crime-free. Its a place where neighbors know each other and are open to socializing

A couple attractions in the area are:

  • Dripping Springs Ranch Park
  • Buda Amphitheater & City Park
  • Ilario's Italian Cuisine
